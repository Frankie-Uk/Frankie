SCHOOL HISTORY

Welcome to my school page... Description of my Primary, Secondary school and University.

Primary School

I attended St Peters Cathedral Anglican school Asaba, Delta state...( 2011-2015 ) It is both a day school and boarding school.
My primary school experience was'nt really the best because it was an Anglican school and I am a catholic so they were
really strict. When it came to my studies, I was'nt really the smartest but I still was'nt the worst. I am the playful type so I didnt
really care about my studies like that but I still ended up in top 15 out of 32. I had very few friends but I remember being very close
with my teachers. Overall, there were still some enjoyable moments and I have really good memories from there.







Secondary School

The name of my secondary school is Noble Academy Asaba, Deta State...( 2015-2020 )It is a day school. My academics is Junior Secondry
School(JSS) gradually started increasing compared to primary school because I ended up being among the top 5. But it slowly started decreasing
again as I advanced to Senior Secondary School (SSS).I usually end up in top 10 because the competion was much but regardless, I never strayed
from understandin what I'm being taught. I am very grateful for my secondary school social experiences, be it making new and wonderful friends
or being verbally bullied, they all taught me something; That humans can be very loyal and supportive.. and also how to clapback at people that
don't mean well for you. As for my academic exerience, The teachrs were nice and they taught me well.









University
